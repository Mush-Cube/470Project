Cookie Coders serves as an organization to enrich the youth through education for the modern world of technology. With our guidance, we will educate the youth on the basics of Java, C++, Python, as well as fun interactive programming such as Fortran 77 and Inform 7. We are hosting a series of 7 day events throughout the United States to target all youth for this great opportunity. Our Game Developer Event offers students the potential of getting a head start in pursuing a career in the booming field of software development.
